**Effective date:** March 14, 2026

We may collect the following types of information:

Some of this data may be stored locally on your device.

### d. Technical Data
We may receive limited technical information required to operate the app, such as:

- Device and platform information

- Basic service logs generated by our backend or hosting providers

We use your information to:

- Create and manage your account
- Let you sign in securely
- Save your profile and preferences
- Provide daily challenges, games, and personalized app features
- Sync your information across supported devices
- Improve app functionality, security, and reliability
- Respond to support or legal requests when required

We do not sell your personal information.

## 3. Profile Information You Provide

The app may allow you to add profile details such as age or an emergency contact. You provide this information voluntarily.

Your data may be stored on secure servers provided by these service providers and on your device.

## 5. Data Sharing

We may share information only in the following situations:

- With service providers that help us operate the app
- If required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

## 6. Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the app.

We may retain certain information for a longer period if required for:

- Legal compliance
- Dispute resolution
- Security and fraud prevention

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- Access the personal information associated with your account
- Correct or update your information
